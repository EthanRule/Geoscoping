## Casing Conventions

> - **.tsx :** PascalCase (e.g., UserProfile.tsx).
> - **.ts :** camelCase (e.g., authService.ts, stringUtils.ts).
> - **dirs :** kebab-case (e.g., user-profiles, auth-services).
